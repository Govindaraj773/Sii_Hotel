About Si! Beach House Hotel

Located on the picturesque island of Dhiffushi, Sii Beach House offers a mesmerising escape where luxury meets the beauty of the Maldives. Surrounded by pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters, our beach house is the perfect retreat for couples, families, and solo travelers alike.


Accommodations

Our spacious and elegantly appointed rooms provide a seamless blend of comfort and style. Each room features modern amenities including plush bedding, air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and private bathrooms.

Step out onto your private terrace to enjoy breathtaking views of the ocean and feel the gentle sea breeze.


Dining

Enjoy delicious meals at our beachfront restaurant. We serve a variety of dishes, from traditional Maldivian cuisine to international favorites. Our menu includes fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and tasty desserts


Activities

Dhiffushi offers many activities for both adventure lovers and those looking to relax. Explore the vibrant coral reefs with snorkeling or diving trips, take a peaceful kayak ride, or try stand-up paddleboarding

For ultimate relaxation, take the evening stroll by the beach and catch those colors of sunset as beautiful like a movie scene!

At Sii Beach House, we aim to make your stay relaxing and memorable, surrounded by the natural beauty of Dhiffushi.
