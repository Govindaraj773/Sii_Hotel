About Sii Coral Inn Hotel

Located on the picturesque island of Thulusdhoo, Sii Coral Inn offers a perfect blend of Maldivian culture and modern comforts. Whether you're here for adventure or relaxation, or perfect honeymoon trip to Maldives our inn provides a serene escape surrounded by the natural beauty of this place.


Accommodations

Our thoughtfully designed rooms offer breathtaking views of the turquoise Indian Ocean and lush island greenery.

Each room features comfortable king-sized beds, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, and en-suite bathrooms with rain showers. Enjoy a refreshing drink on your private balcony while soaking in the tranquil atmosphere.


Dining

Treat yourself at our oceanfront restaurant, where our chefs create delicious local and international dishes with the freshest ingredients. From tropical fruits to freshly caught seafood, every meal is a delight.

Our beachside balcony rooms are perfect for having a moment to yourself as you watch the sunset.


Activities

    Thulusdhoo is famous for its vibrant coral reefs and exciting water sports. Dive into the underwater world with snorkeling and diving trips, or catch some waves with our surfing packages.

    For a more relaxing experience, unwind with a rejuvenating treatment at our spa, inspired by traditional Maldivian practices

    At Sii Coral Inn, we aim to provide a memorable and relaxing stay, surrounded by the stunning beauty of Thulusdhoo.
